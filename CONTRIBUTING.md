# First and foremost, due to the nature of the project we will NOT be accepting pull requests.
The Icebreaker Project is part of the Capstone Graduation project of a few studentsfrom [Fairchild Wheeler Interdistrict Magnet Highschool](https://www.fairchildwheeler.org), who shall remain Anonymous under their usernames for personal reasons.
As a school project, we are not allowed to accept code written by other people not affiliated with the project or our school.

## This does not mean that you cannot help.
You are free to download the app (builds will be on the releases page) and try it and to submit issue reports as necessary. These do not conflict with the policy of no outside help, and are very welcome and appreciated.
